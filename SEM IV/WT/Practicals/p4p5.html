DEPSTAR ENTREPRENEURSHIP & COMMUNICATION CLUB

Devang Patel Institute of Advance Technology and Research (DEPSTAR) always inspires students for the overall development in academics and co-curricular activities. The Entrepreneurship Communication club was started with the aim to development the entrepreneurship skills of the students. The events organized by the club helps students get idea about how to incubate a business. Along with business skills, communication skills are necessary to run the business. As the name suggests, the Entrepreneurship Communication club, also organizes events like Spell-Bee, Elocution and Debate Competitions for language development and improving the communication skills.
